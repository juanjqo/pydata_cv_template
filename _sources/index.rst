========
About me
========

I am a Research Associate in Legged Robots with the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at
The University of Manchester. I am currently based at RAICo1.

I have a B.Sc.(2014) In Electronic Engineering. I received my M.Sc. (2017) and my Ph.D (2021)
in Electrical engineering from the Federal University of Minas Gerais (UFMG).
I was a project researcher at The Harada Laboratory at University of Tokyo..
